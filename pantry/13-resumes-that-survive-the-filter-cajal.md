## Figure 1 — The Fork Before the Human
Priority: Critical · Type: process flowchart

BLOCK 1 — ILLUSTRAE PASTE BLOCK:
Render a two-path process flowchart showing that a résumé's fate is decided by a parser before any human sees it. From a single submit node, split into two parallel downward paths.
