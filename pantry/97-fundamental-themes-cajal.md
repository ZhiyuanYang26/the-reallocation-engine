Priority: Important · Type: process flowchart

BLOCK 1 — ILLUSTRAE PASTE BLOCK:

Begin with a node representing encountering material that does not fit prior knowledge — a prediction error. Flow rightward through a chain of neurobiological events: a signaling burst, an upregulation step, synaptic strengthening, and the formation of new connections — terminating in durable memory. Then draw a second parallel lower track showing the AI-removes-struggle path: the same starting situation flows into a remove-friction node which severs the chain, so the downstream consolidation events never fire and durable learning does not form. The contrast must make clear that removing the trigger removes the consolidation.
